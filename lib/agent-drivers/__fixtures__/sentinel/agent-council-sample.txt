## Council Member: Gemini

**Topic:** Should the multi-turn pump use a polling interval or event-driven stdout streaming?

**Analysis:**
Event-driven stdout streaming avoids the latency floor imposed by polling. The PoC confirmed opencode emits step_finish synchronously at turn boundary. Polling would add 200–500 ms median delay with no benefit.

**Risk:** Buffered stdout in some shell environments may coalesce events. Mitigated by line-buffered reads already in runOnce.

## Position
APPROVE

Proceed with event-driven stdout streaming as specified. No further design changes needed before Wave 2 implementation.
